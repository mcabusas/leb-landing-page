COVID-19 Health Safety

During these unsure occasions, we have a considerably more prominent obligation to every one of you.
We will give it our best shot to protect our locale.

SAFETY MEASURES FOR

OUR CUSTOMERS

Here are some of the ways to better serve you as our country adjust to this new normal:

1. Practice social distancing in accepting your order from riders.
2. Wash your hands with soap right after getting the order.
3. When not at home, always wear a mask.
4. Stay at home.
To furthur ensure your safety, you can decrease your contact by going cashless in your payment.

SAFETY MEASURES FOR

OUR MERCHANTS

To ensure the safety of our users, here are some preventive measures we encourage merchants to practice.
1. All staff must wear their masks at all times.
2. Place a designated area for pick up orders in practicing social distancing from riders to your staff.
3. Always wash hands with soap and water before giving the food.
4. Wrap the food properly..
5. Do temperature checks of your staff.

SAFETY MEASURES FOR

OUR RIDERS

1. Wear your mask when on the job at all times.
2. Make sure to not put your belongings and the item in the same delivery bag or anywhere close to the item.

3. While waiting at the store for pick up, practice social distancing within the premise or wait outside until the order is ready for delivery.
4. Frequently clean and disinfect your delivery bags.

5. Wash your hands with soap and water or sanitize before picking up or available.
To furthur ensure your safety, you can decrease your contact by going cashless in your payment.
